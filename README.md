Insights:

- Total searches reached 138,638 across the year
- Arrest conversion rate of 17.6% means roughly 1 in 6 searches led to an arrest
- Controlled drugs dominate heavily at 80,328 searches (57.9%) — the clear primary driver
- Offensive weapons at 18,826 (13.6%) — notable but far behind drugs, suggesting stop and search is not primarily a "knife crime" tool in raw volume terms
- Searches are relatively stable most months (~10,800–12,400), with a clear outlier in August, possibly due to school summer holidays
